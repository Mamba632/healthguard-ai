About HealthConnect

One place for symptom awareness, self-care, and nearby care search.

HealthConnect is a personal healthcare web experience built to make first-step guidance easier to understand and faster to use.

Clear Guidance

Common symptoms are matched with simple guidance, severity awareness, and practical next steps.

Nearby Care

Users can search hospitals, clinics, pharmacies, emergency care, and diagnostic centers through map search.

Self-Care Library

Listed symptoms connect to focused self-care videos and awareness resources.

Built For

Students, families, elderly users, and anyone who wants a clearer starting point when common health symptoms appear.

Future Roadmap

Backend-protected APIs, appointment storage, Gemini chatbot guidance, login, and real doctor consultation can be added later.
